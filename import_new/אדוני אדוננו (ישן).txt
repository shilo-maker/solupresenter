Title: אדוני אדוננו (ישן)

אדוני אדוננו מה אדיר שמך

adonai adonenu, ma adir shimkha

O Lord, our Lord, how majestic is your name

בְּכָל הָאָרֶץ, אֲשֶׁר תְּנָה הוֹדְךָ עַל הַשָׁמָיִם

bekhol haarets asher tena hodkha al hashamaim

in all the earth!
You have set your glory above the heavens.

מִפִּי עוֹלְלִים וְיוֹנְקִים

mipi olelim veyonkim

Out of the mouth of babies and infants,

יִסַּדְתָּ עוֹז

yisadeta oz

You have established strength

לְמַעַן צוֹרְרֶיךָ

lema'an tsorerekha

because of Your foes

לְהַשְׁבִּית אוֹיֵב וּמִתְנַקֵּם

lehashbit oyev umitnakem

  to still the enemy and the avenger

 כִּי אֶרְאֶה שָׁמֶיךָ

ki ereh shamekha

When I look at your heavens

מַעֲשֵׂה אֶצְבְּעוֹתֶיךָ,

maaseh etsbeotekha

the work of your fingers

יָרֵח וְכוֹכָבִים אֲשֶׁר כּוֹנָנְתָּה.

yare'akh vekhokhavim asher konanta

the moon and the stars, which you have set in place

מָה אֱנוֹשׁ כִּי תִּזְכְּרֶנוּ

ma enosh ki tizkerenu

what is man that you are mindful of him

וּבֵן אָדָם כִּי תִּפְקְדֶנוּ,

uven adam ki tifkedenu

 and the son of man that you care for him?

וַתְּחַסְּרֵהוּ מְעַט מֵאֱלוֹהִים,

vatekhasrehu me'at me'elohim

Yet you have made him a little lower than the heavenly beings

וְכָבוֹד וְהָדָר תְּעַטְּרֵהוּ

vekhavod vehadar te'atrehu

and crowned him with glory and honor

תַּמְשִׁילֵהוּ בְּמַעֲשֵׂי יָדֶיךָ

tamshilehu bema'aseh yadekha

You have given him dominion over the works of your hands

כָּל שַׁתָּה תַּחַת רַגְלָיו

kol shata takhat raglav

    you have put all things under his feet

צונה ואלפים כולם

tsoneh ve'alafim kulam

all sheep and oxen

וְגַם בַּהֲמוֹת שדי

vegam bahamot shaday

    and also the beasts of the field

 צִפּוֹר שָׁמַיִם, וּדְגֵי הַיָּם

tsipor shamaim, udgey hayam

the birds of the heavens, and the fish of the sea

עוֹבֵר אָרְחוֹת -יַמִּים

over orkhot yamim

    whatever passes along the paths of the seas
