Title: אהבתו גדולה

אַהֲבָתוֹ גְּדוֹלָה

ahavato gdola

His love is great

כִּי דַּם יֵשׁוּעַ נִשְׁפָּךְ

ki dam Yeshua nishfah

because Yeshua's blood was spilled

אוהב אוֹתוֹ, אַהֲלֵל אֶת שְׁמו

ohav oto ahalel et shmo veagid tehilato

I will love Him, I will praise his name

וְאַגִיד תְּהִילָתוֹ, לְעוֹלָם, לְעוֹלָם

veagid tehilato le'olam, leolam

and declare his praise forever
