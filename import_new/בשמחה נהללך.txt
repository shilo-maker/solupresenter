Title: בשמחה נהללך

 בְּשִׂמְחָה נְהַלֲלֶך, אֵל שׁוֹפֵעַ רַחֲמִים

besimkha nehalelekha el shofe'a rakhamim

Joyful, joyful, we adore Thee,
God of glory, Lord of love;

כִּפְרָחִים – כָּל לִבּוֹתֵינוּ לְפָנֵיךָ נִפְתָּחִים

kifrakhim kol libotenu lefanekha niftakhim

Hearts unfold like flow’rs before Thee,
Op’ning to the sun above.

עַנְנֵי הַחֵטְא הָמֵס נָא, אֲפֵלַת-סָפֵק מִתְּהוֹם

anane hakhet hames na afelat safem mthom

Melt the clouds of sin and sadness;
Drive the dark of doubt away;

הֲצִיפֵנוּ אוֹשֶׁר נֶצַח, וּמַלְּאֵנוּ זוֹהַר-יוֹם

hatsifenu osher netsakh umalenu zohar yom

Giver of immortal gladness,
Fill us with the light of day!

בְּשִׂמְחָה יְסוֹבְבוּךָ פָּעֳלֶיךָ הַדְּגוּלִים

bsimkha yesovevukha pe'alekha hadgulim

All Thy works with joy surround Thee,
Earth and heav’n reflect Thy rays

אֶרֶץ, יָם, וּתְכוֹל רָקִיעַ, כּוֹכָבִים וּמַלְאָכִים

erets yam utkhol rakia, kokhavim umalakhim

Stars and angels sing around Thee,
Center of unbroken praise.

נִיר וָיַעַר, גַּיְא וָגֶבַע,

nir vaya'ar gai vegeva

Field and forest, vale and mountain,

חוֹל מִדְבָּר וּמֵי הַיָּם

khol midbar ume hayam

Flow’ry meadow, flashing sea

שִׁיר צִפּוֹר, הֶמְיַית מַבּוּעַ –

shir tsipor hmiat mabua

Singing bird and flowing fountain

יְשַׁבְּחוּ שִׁמְךָ הָרָם

yeshabkhu shimkha haram

Call us to rejoice in Thee

בְּנֵי תְמוּתָה הָרִיעוּ יַחַד 

bne tmuta hariu yakhad

Mortals, join the happy chorus,

עִם צִבְאוֹת הַמַּלְאֲכִים

 im tsva'ot hamalakhim

Which the morning stars began;

אַהֲבַת הָאֵל מוֹשֶׁלֶת, וְתִמְשׁוֹל לְעוֹלָמִים

ahavat ha'el moshelet vetimshol le'olamim

Father love is reigning o’er us,
Brother love binds man to man.

בְּשִׁירָה נִצְעַד קָדִימָהָ

beshira nitsad kadima 

Ever singing, march we onward,

מְנַצְּחִים בְּנִצְחוֹנְךָ

 menatskhim bnitskhonkha

Victors in the midst of strife,

מַנְגִינוֹת הַלֵּל תַּנְעֵמְנָה אֶת דַּרְכֵּנוּ אֶל דְּבִירְךָ

manginot halel tn'emna et darkenu el dvirkha

Joyful music leads us Sunward
In the triumph song of life.
