Title: גדול אלוהי

גדול אלוהי

gadol Elohai

How great is our God

שירו כי גדול אלוהי

shiru ki gadol Elohai

Sing with me how great is our God

כל אחד יראה כי גדול אלוהי

kol ekhad ireh ki gadol Elohai

Everyone will see, how great is our God

שם מעל כל שם, אתה ראוי להלל

shem me'al kol shem ata raui le'halel

Name above all names 
You are worthy of all praise

ליבי ישיר כי גדול אלוהי

libi yashir ki gadol Elohai

My heart will sing how great is our God

الرب في علاه مكللاً ببهاه

Arrabbu fe Eulah mukallan fe baha’

The splendor of a King, clothed in majesty

عظيم القدرة شديد الرفعة

Arrabbu fe Eulah mukallan fe baha’

Let all the Earth rejoice


Yaltaffu belmajdi walthulma takhtafi

يلتف بالمجد والظلمة تختفي

He wraps himself in light, And darkness tries to hide

من وجه ذا الإله من وجه ذا الإله

Min wajhi tha elilah min wajhi thal ilah

And trembles at His voice

من جيل لجيل يملك والكل سيخضع

Min jeel lijeel yamlukoo walkullo sayakhda’Eu

Age to age He stands And time is in His hands

للألف والياء الكائن للأبد

Lil’Alifi walya’ alka’en lel’abad

Beginning and the end

الأب السماوي الابن السرمدي

Al’abbu elsama’i al’ibnu elsarmadi

The Godhead Three in One
Father Spirit Son

الروح الأزلي الروح الأزلي

AlruuHu el’Azali AlruuHu el’Azali 

The Lion and the Lamb

How  great is our God

Kam anta Eatheem ya allah
