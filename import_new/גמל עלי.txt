Title: גמל עלי

Gamal alay, gamal alay berov khasdo
-
He has been good to me, with all His grace

Lo kepsha’ay gamal li X3
Akh lefi khasdo
-
Not according to my sins He treated me
But according to His grace

Hu abba tov vehu gomel tov
-
He’s a good father, and He rewards with goodness

Ani ben shel melekh akhshav X3
Ki hu gamal alay
-
I am a son of the King now
For He has been good to me
