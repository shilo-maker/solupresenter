Title: הללי נפשי

 אֲהַלְלָה אֲדוֹנָי בְּחַיָּי
אֲזַמְרָה לְאֱלוֹהַי בְּעוֹדִי


Ahalela Adonai Bechayai
Azamera leElohai Beodi

I will worship the Lord in my life
I will sing to the Lord with my being

הַלְלִי נַפְשִׁי אֶת אֲדוֹנָי 

Haleli Nafshi et Adonai

Praise the Lord oh my soul


יִמְלוֹךְ אֲדוֹנָי לְעוֹלָם, הַלְלוּיָהּ
אֱלוֹהַיִיךְ צִיּוֹן, לְדוֹר וָדוֹר


Yimloch Adonai Leolam, Haleluya
Elohaich Tsion, ledor vador

The Lord will reign forever, Haleluya
Your God Zion, from generation to generation



בְּחַיָּי, אֲזַמְרָה לְאֱלוֹהַי

Bechayai, Azamera leElohai

In my life, I will praise the Lord
