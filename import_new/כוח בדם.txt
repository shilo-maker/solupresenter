Title: כוח בדם

אִם תְּחַפֵּשׂ הַשִּׁחְרוּר מֵעָוֹן

I'm these hashihrur me'avon

Would you be free from the burden of sin?

יֵשׁ כּוֹח בַּדָּם, כּוֹח בַּדָּם

yesh koakh badam, koakh badam

There’s pow’r in the blood, pow’r in the blood

אִם תְּחַפֵּשׂ עַל הַחֵטְא נִצָּחוֹן

im tehapes al hahet nitsahon

Would you o’er evil a victory win?

יֵשׁ כּוֹח נִפְלָא רַק בַּדָּם

yesh koan nifla rak badam

There’s wonderful pow’r in the blood.

כּוֹח, כּוֹח, כּוֹח יֵשׁ נִפְלָא רַק בְּדָם הָנִּדְקָר;

{koah X3} yesh nifla rak badam hanidkar

There is pow’r, pow’r, wonder-working pow’r
In the blood of the Lamb

כּוֹח כּוֹח כּוֹח יֵשׁ נִפְלָא רַק בְּדָם הַשֶׂה הַיָּקָר.

{koah X3} iesh nifla rak badam hase haiakar

There is pow’r, pow’r, wonder-working pow’r
In the precious blood of the Lamb

אִם תְּחַפֵּשׂ הַשִּׁחְרוּר מִטֻּמְאָה

I'm these hashihrur mituma

bo hitrahets bema'aian golgota

Come for a cleansing to Calvary’s tide

yesh koan nifla rak badam

There’s wonderful pow’r in the blood.

אִם גַם כַּשֶּׁלֶג תִּרְצֶה לְהַלְבִּין

im gam kasheleg tirtse lehalbin

Would you be whiter, much whiter than snow?

yesh koakh badam, koakh badam

כֶּתֶם הַחֵטְא בִּזְרָמָיו יֵעָלֵם

ketem hahet bzramav ie'alem

Sin-stains are lost in its life-giving flow;

yesh koan nifla rak badam

אִם לְיֵשׁוּעַ תִּרְצֶה לַעֲבוֹד

im leYeshua tirtse la'avod

Would you do service for Jesus your King?

ubamashiah laad laamod

Would you live daily His praises to sing?
