Title: לאל התפארת

לאל התּפארת על כּל מעשׂיו,

la'el hatiferet al kol maasav

To God be the glory, for great things He has done

כּי את עוֹלמנוּ הוּא כּל-כּך אָהב,

ki et olamenu hu kolkakh ahav

So loved He the world 

עד כּי בּנוֹ שׁלח לכפּר חטאים,

ad ki bno shalakh lekhaper khataim

that He gave us His Son, who yielded His life our redemption to win

ושׁער פּתח לחיּי עוֹלמים

vesha'ar patakh lekhayey olamim

And opened the life-gate that all may go in

"שׁיר הלּל! שׁיר לאל!" יזמּר העוֹלם!

shir halel, shir la'el yezamer ha'olam

Praise the Lord, praise the Lord,
Let the earth hear His voice;

"שׁיר הלּל! שׁיר לאל!" יענוּ בּני אָדם.

shir halel, shir la'el ya'anu bney adam

Praise the Lord, praise the Lord, let the people rejoice;

קראוּ אל ישׁוּע וּבוֹאוּ לאָב,

kiru el Yeshua ubou la'av

Oh, come to the Father, through Jesus the Son,

ורוֹנּוּ לוֹ זמר על כּל מעשׂיו.

veronu lo zemer al kol maasav

And give Him the glory; great things He hath done.

שׁלמה הגּאלּה וּקנוּיה בּדמים; 

shlema hageula uknuya bedamim

Oh, perfect redemption, the purchase of blood,


לכל מאמין – הבטחת אלוֹהים:

lekol maamin havtakhat elohim

To every believer the promise of God;

אַף גּדוֹל הפּוֹשׁעים, אם ישׁוּב מפּשעיו 

af gdol haposhim im yashuv mipshaav

The vilest offender who truly believes

בּרגע ההוּא יִסּלחוּ חטאָיו!

berega hahu isalkhu khataav

That moment from Jesus a pardon receives.

גּדוֹלה תּוֹרתוֹ, עצוּמים מעשׂיו,

gdola torato atsumim maasav

Great things He hath taught us, great things He hath done,

רבּה שׂמחתנוּ בּבּן וּבאָב.

raba simkhatenu baben uba'av

And great our rejoicing through Jesus the Son;

אבל שׁבעתיִם אָשׁרנוּ יִרבּה

aval shivataim oshrenu irbe

But purer, and higher, and greater will be

עת פּני מוֹשׁיענוּ, ישׁוּע, נראה

et pney moshienu Yeshua nireh!

Our wonder, our transport when Jesus we see.
