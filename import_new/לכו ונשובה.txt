Title: לכו ונשובה

לְכוּ וְנָשׁוּבָה אֶל-יְהוָה

lekhu venashuva el adonai

Come, let us return to the Lord

כִּי הוּא טָרָף וְיִרְפָּאֵנוּ

ki hu taraf va'irpa'enu

for He has torn us, that He may heal us;

יַךְ, וְיַחְבְּשֵׁנוּ

yakh veyakhbeshenu

He has struck us down, and he will bind us up

יְחַיֵּנוּ, מִיֹּמָיִם

yekhayenu miomain

After two days he will revive us;

בַּיּוֹם, הַשְּׁלִישִׁי, יְקִמֵנוּ

bayom hashlishi yekimenu

on the third day he will raise us up,

יְחַיֵּנוּ, מִיֹּמָיִם

yekhayenu miomaim

After two days he will revive us;

וְנִחְיֶה לְפָנָיו

venikhyeh lefanav

that we may live before him.

וְנֵדְעָה נִרְדְּפָה

veneda birdefa

Let us know; let us press on 

לָדַעַת אֶת-יְהוָה

lada'at et Adonai

to know the Lord
