Title: נשמת כל חי

נִשְׁמַת כּל חַי תִּשְׁתַּחֲוֶוה

nishmat kol khai tishtakhave

The soul of every living thing will bow

תִּכְרַע כָּל בֶּרֶךְ, כָּל לָשׁוֹן תּוֹדֶה 

tikhra kol berekh kol lashon tode

before the Lord and every tounge shall confess

כִּי אַתָּה מֶלֶך הַמָּשִׁיחַ

ki ata melekh hamashiakh

Because you are the Messiah king

לְתִּפְארת אֳלוֹהִים הַאָב

letiferet elohim ha'av

to the glory of father God

יֵשׁוּעַ הַמָשִׁיח - לְבַדְּךָ אַתָּה אָדוֹן

Yeshua hamashiakh levadkha ata adon

Yeshua Messiah, only you are Lord
