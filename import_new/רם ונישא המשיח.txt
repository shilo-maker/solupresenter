Title: רם ונישא המשיח

רָם וְנִשָׂא הַמָּשִׁיחַ

ram venisa hamashiakh

High and exalted is the messiah

הוֹד וְהָדָר לְבוּשׁוֹ

hod vehadar levusho

he is clothed in glory and splendor

כָּאן בְּלִבִּי הַמָּשִׁיחַ

kan belibi hamashiakh

Here in my heart

שׁוֹכֵן בְּהֵיכָלוֹ

shokhen behekhalo

the messiah dwells in His holy place

עָלָה לַמִּזְבֵּחַ כְּשֶׂה

ala lamizbe'akh kese

He ascended to the alter as a lamb

עָנְשִׁי הוּא שִׁילֵם כְּפוֹדֶה

onshi hu shilem kefode

He paid my punishment as my redeemer

כּל בֶּרֶךְ תִּכְרַע

kol berekh tikhra

Every knee will bow

כּל לָשׁוֹן כֵּן תּוֹדֶה

kol lashon, ken tode

and every tongue will confess

הִשְׁתַּחֲווּ לְפָנָיו

hishtakhavu lefanav

Bow before Him, before the one 

הַיוֹשֵׁב לִימִינוֹ שֶׁל הָאָב

hayoshev leimino shel ha'av

who sits to the right of the father

כּל עַיִן תִּרְאֶה

kol ain tire

Every eye will see

שִׁיבָתוֹ לִבְחִירָיו

shivato livkhirav

His return to His elect
