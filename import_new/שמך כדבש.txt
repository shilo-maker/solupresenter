Yeshua holy and anointed

ישוע, ישוע 

Yeshua, Yeshua,

Yeshua, Yeshua,

נישא מעל השמיים, ישוע

nisa me'al hashamaim Yeshua

shimha kedvash lesfatai

ruheha kemaim lenafshi

dvarha kener leraglai

your word like a lamp to my feet

 eshtahave leha, Yeshua
