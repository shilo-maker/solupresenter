Title: Av Ha'rakhamim - Father Of Mercy Test

אב הבדיקה, אלוהי החסד

av ha'rakhamim, Elohei ha'khesed

Father of mercy, God of grace

מלך המלכים, שומע תפילה

melekh ha'mlakhim, shomea tfila

King of Kings, hearer of prayers

אלוהי כל נשמה, אבינו קדוש שמך

Elohei kol nshama, avinu kadosh shimkha

God of every soul, 
Our father Holy is Your name
