Title: אל ואדון

אֵל וְאָדוֹן, שׁוֹכֵן בְּסֵתֶר רַעַם

el ve'adon shokhen beseter ra'am

O Lord my God, when I in awesome wonder

בְּרוֹב פְּלִיאָה אַבִּיט בְּפָעָלְךָ

berov plia abit befo'alkha

Consider all the worlds Thy hands have made

אָשׁוּר כּוֹכְבֵי שְׁחָקִים אֲשֶׁר בָּרָאתָּ

ashur kokhve shkhakim asher barata

I see the stars, I hear the rolling thunder

עֵדִים חַיִּים לְנִפְלְאוֹת יָדְךָ

edim khaim lenifla'ot yadkha

Thy power throughout the universe displayed

אָז יַעַל שִׁיר בְּשֶׁבַח גְּדֻלָּתְךָ

az ya'al shir beshevakh gdulatkha

Then sings my soul, my Savior God, to Thee

"אַדִּיר שִׁמְּךָ, נִשְׂגָּב אַתָּה!"

adir shimkha, nisgav ata

How great Thou art! How great Thou art!

יַעַל שִׁירִי אֶל כֵּס תִּפְאַרְתְּךָ

ya'al shiri el kes tifartekha

Then sings my soul, My Savior God, to Thee

"נִשָּׂא וָרָם, קָדוֹשׁ אַתָּה!"

nisa varam kadosh ata

How great Thou art! How great Thou art!

בִּסְבַךְ הַחוֹרֶש, בָּעֳבִי הַיַּעַר

bisvakh hakhoresh be'ave haya'ar

When through the woods, and forest glades I wander

אֶשְׁמַע צִפּוֹר פּוֹצַחַת שִׁיר חֶדְוָה

eshma tsipor potsakhat shir khedva

And hear the birds sing sweetly in the trees

מַשַׁב צַפְרִיר מֵעַל פִּסְגַת הַגֶּבַע

mashav tsafrir me'al pisgat hageva

When I look down, from lofty mountain grandeur

שִׁכְשׁוּךְ הַפֶּלֶג – אָץ לוֹ בִּמְשׁוּבָה

shikhshukh hapeleg ats lo bimshuva

And hear the brook, and feel the gentle breeze

וְאָז אֶזְכּוֹר: הָאֵל חָנוּן רַב חֶסֶד

ve'az ezkor ha'el khanun rav khesed

And when I think, that God, His Son not sparing

בְּנוֹ לא חָשַׂךְ מִכּוֹבֶד אַשְׁמָתִי

bno lo khasakh mikoved ashmati

Sent Him to die, I scarce can take it in;

עַל עֵץ הַצְּלָב מַשָּׂא פִּשְׁעִי עָמַס הוּא

al ets hatslav masa pishi amas hu

That on the cross, my burden gladly bearing,

סָבַל וָמֵת, כְּכוֹפֶר נִשְׁמָתִי

saval vamet, kekofer nishmati

He bled and died to take away my sin.

וְכַאֲשֶׁר תָּשׁוּב, פּוֹדִי אֶל אֶרֶץ

veka'asher tashuv podi el arets

When Christ shall come, with shout of acclamation,

וּבְקוֹל תְּרוּעָה אוֹתִי אֶל-עַל תִּשָּׂא

ubkokl trua oti el al tisa

And take me home, what joy shall fill my heart

אֶשְׁתַּחֲוֶה בְּרוֹב יִרְאָה וָפֶלֶא

eshtakhave berov ira vafele

Then I shall bow, in humble adoration,

וַאֲבָרְכָה: "עֶלְיוֹן, נִשְׂגָּב אַתָּה

ve'avarkha: elyon nisgav ata

And then proclaim, "My God, how great Thou art!"
