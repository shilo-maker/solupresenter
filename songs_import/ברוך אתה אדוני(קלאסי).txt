Title: ברוך אתה אדוני(קלאסי)

בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה אֲדוֹנָי, אֱלוֹהֵי יִשְׂרָאֵל אָבִינוּ.

baruh ata adonai, elohe israel avinu

Blessed are you Lord, God of Israel our father

בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה אֲדוֹנָי, מֵעוֹלָם וְעַד עוֹלָם

baruh ata adonai, meolam vead olam

Blessed are you Lord, God of Israel our father, from age to age

לְךָ הַגְּדֻלָה לְךָ הַגְּבוּרָה, לְךָ הַתִּפְאֶרֶת אֲדוֹנָי

leha agdula leha agvura leha atifert adonai

To you be the greatness,
 the might and the glory

לְךָ אֲדוֹנָי הַנֵּצַח,

leha adonai hanetsah

to you be eternity

 לְךָ הַהוֹד וְהֶהָדָר, אֲדוֹנָי

 leha hahod vehahadar adonai

 all the glory and the honour, Lord

בְּיָדֶיךָ כּח, בְּיָדֶיךָ גְּבוּרָה

beiadeha koan beiadeha gvura 

In your hands the is strength and power

לְגַדֵּל וּלְחַזֵּק לַכּל

legadel ulehzek lakol,

to strengthen and empower

אַתָּה אֲדוֹנָי מוֹשֵל בַּכּל,

ata adonai moshel bakol

you reign above all

לְךָ אֲדוֹנָי הַמַּמְלָכָה, הַמַּמְלָכָה.

leha adonai hamamlaha, hamamlaha

you reign above all, to you be the kingdom
