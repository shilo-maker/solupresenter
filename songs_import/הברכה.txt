Title: The Blessing (TRANSLATED)

יברכך יהוה וישמרך

yevarekhekha Adonai ve'ishmerekha

THE LORD BLESS YOU
AND KEEP YOU

יאר יהוה פניו אליך ויחונך

yaer Adonai panav elekha ve'yikhunekha

may his face shine upon you
and be gracious to you

ישא יהוה פניו אליך

yisa Adonai panav eleikha

THE LORD TURN HIS FACE TOWARDS YOU

וישם לך שלום

ve'yasem lekha shalom

AND GIVE YOU PEACE

אמן, אמן

amen amen

AMEN, AMEN

אמן, אמן

amen amen

AMEN, AMEN

יהי חסדו עליך

yehi khasdo aleikha

MAY HIS FAVOR BE UPON YOU

לאלפיי דורות

le'alfei dorot

AND A THOUSAND GENERATIONS

משפחתיך, ילדיך

mishpakhteikha, yeladeikha

YOUR FAMILY AND YOUR CHILDREN

דורותיך אחריך

dorotekha akharekha

AND THEIR CHILDREN
AND THEIR CHILDREN

רוחו תלך לפניך

rukhu telekh le'faneikha

may his presence go before you

מסביבך, בליבך

mi'svivekha, be'libekha

and behind you and beside you

הוא איתך, הוא איתך

hu itkha, hu itkha

HE IS WITH YOU, HE IS WITH YOU

בבוקר ובערב

ba'boker u'ba'erev

IN THE MORNING AND THE EVENING

בצאתך ובואך

be'tzetekha u'voekha

IN YOUR COMING AND YOUR GOING

בסבלותיך ואושריך

be'sivlotekha ve'osrekha

in your weeping and rejoicing

הוא איתך, הוא איתך

hu itkha, hu itkha

HE is For YOU, HE is for YOU

הוא איתנו, עם כולנו

hu itanu, im kulanu

HE IS WITH US, HE IS WITH US
