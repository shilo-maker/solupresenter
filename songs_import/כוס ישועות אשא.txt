Title: כוס ישועות אשא

מָה אָשִׁיב לַאֲדוֹנָי כָּל תַּגְמוּלָיו עָלָי

ma ashiv la'adonai kol tagmolav alay

What shall I return to the Lord
for all his goodness to me?

כּוֹס יְשׁוּעוֹת אֶשָּׂא, וּבְשֵׁם אֲדוֹנָי אֶקְרָא.

kos yeshu'ot esa ubeshem adonai ekra

I will lift up the cup of salvation
and call on the name of the Lord.

לְךָ אֶזְבַּח זֶבַח תּוֹדָה

lekha ezbakh zevakh toda

I will sacrifice a thank offering to you

 וּבְשֵׁם אֲדוֹנָי אֶקְרָא

ubeshem adonai ekra

and call on the name of the Lord.

נְדָרַי לַאֲדוֹנָי, נְדָרַי אֲשַׁלֵּם

nedarai la'adonai nedarai ashalem

I will fulfill my vows to the Lord
