Title: קדוש שמך לעד

כי גדול אתה וקדוש שמך לעד

ki gadol ata ve'kadosh shimkha la'ad

for great are you and your name is holy forever

טוב להודות לאדוני ולזמר לשמך עליון

tove lehodot ladonai ulezmer leshemkha elyon

it is good to give thanks to the lord and sing praises to his name 

להגיד חסדך כי גדול אתה וקדוש שמך

lehagid khasdekha ki gadol ata ve'kadosh shimkha

to tell of your mercies for great are you and holy is ypur name
